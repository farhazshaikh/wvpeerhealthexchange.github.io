Westview Peer Health Exchange

Through field trips to hospitals and operation rooms, helping kids battling cancer and diseases, fundraising for medicial research and clinical trials, Westview Peer Health Exchange hopes to inspire Westview Students into the field of medicine.

  • Meet the Team

Farhaz Shaikh

President

An enthusiastic and driven student, Farhaz Shaikh founded Westview Peer Health Exchange. Shaikh is a dedicated peer counselor and creator of Saving our Senses, a non-profit organization devoted to raising hearing aids for low-income hearing impaired kids in developing countries. He has spent most of his summer in the lab of Alere, helping various scientists on medical topics. As a kid, Shaikh spent his time in the hospital battling a disease and has a vivid memory of doctors conducting clinical trials to find the perfect treatment. As a result of his time at the hospital, Farhaz became driven to inspire a generation of doctors.
